Bully Blocker

This app is suitable for any age from young children to adults. It Is all about stopping cyberbullying on anything you receive on a phone. You can customize your settings, so that parents have control over the messages their children are receiving and so they know what is going on. Bully blocker is an amazing app as it avoids bullying which could lead to depression, anxiety and mental health issues. You can also have settings so that their child doesn’t know the app has been installed (optional). When you receive a harmful message it will automatically blocks the sender and notifies them that their message has not delivered.
