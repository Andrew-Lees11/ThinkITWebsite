Safari Grand National   

The Safari Grand National is a charity event to help raise money for the protection and release of the endangered animals in captivity . At the Grand National there will be several different animal races. Each race you watch will cost £5 for a child, £10 a 15-adult, £7.50 for a senior, Under 5s go free . All money made will go to the charity to realise the animals into the wild.
