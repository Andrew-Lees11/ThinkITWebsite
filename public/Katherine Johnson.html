Cropters

Cropters are an easy, stress-free solution to caring for all of your crops. It allows you to have some well-deserved free time or do other work while the cropter waters and checks on your fields. The cropter has a handy camera which allows you to see your crop from the comfort of your sofa! Many of its other uses lets people who cannot leave their houses to see the world outside from a breathtaking view. In addition to its waterproof qualities, it uses eco-friendly solar power to run, even at night. It's only £14.99 to rent for a day!
