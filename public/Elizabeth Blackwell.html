Planning Partner



What is Planning Partner?

Planning Partner is a fun and easy to use app that helps motivate and organise your daily school/work routine. It provides features such as a diary, school map and an alarm clock. It also features daily challenges to aim to achieve during the school day. This effective method of organisation is proved to help motivate students and make school more enjoyable.
