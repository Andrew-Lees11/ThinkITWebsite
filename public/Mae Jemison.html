What is Tech-Tonic

The Tech-Tonic presents a brand new, luxury, State of the art, Life saving Watch.

It has a button which sends a signal to an Emergency Service with your Current Location when you're caught up in or involved in a Natural disaster or Situation.

A satelite uses your location to view the situation at that exact moment.

It is finger ID Activated and it also has a Fully Functioning Watch.

The Tech-Tonic's New watch can be rented for £19.99 a month or £199.99 To purchase for Life. The watch always comes with a Lifetime Guarantee.
