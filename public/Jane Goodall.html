About our product

Helping hands can be used inside or outside for cleaning or even in the field just by the simple press of a button or some keys.

These solar powered robots come with a charger in case there’s no sun out.

They’re all made out of recycled metals and are shipped in pairs with spare parts and as a bonus doesn’t make a noise.

It can hold 500kg and you can get it in gold or silver.
